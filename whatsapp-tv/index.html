About WhatsApp Tv

WhatsApp TV is a WhatsApp feature that helps entertain WhatsApp users. In other words, it is a platform that can be used to make your followers lively by posting interesting kinds of stuff like short, funny videos, memes, stories, and other stuff that includes and is not limited to advertising certain goods or services via your status. So, it’s just a platform created for businesses to engage their audience.

With a Whatsapp business account, you can start making money by helping different businesses promote their products on your Whatsapp TV, participating in an affiliate program or referral program, creating an online class, etc.

What’s Required to Start a WhatsApp TV 

To start a WhatsApp TV successfully, there are certain key requirements you have to put into place before jumping to create a WhatsApp TV. These are…

  • Business Niche
  • Android/iOS Device
  • WhatsApp Business Apk
  • Registered Phone Number
  • Brand Name & Logo
  • Image & Video Editing apks
  • Backup (Gmail or iCloud)

1. Discover Your Niche

Discovering a business Niche before deciding to start a business is very important. This should be the very first thing you should think about and decide on before starting any business. You can engage your viewers through memes, jokes, motivational quotes, religious quotes, educational posts, news, updates on how to make money, etc.

A good tip for finding your niche is this: What do you love to discuss? You don’t want to get bored talking about things that don’t interest you at all. Find a topic that you can spend hours reading up on and doing research on, and you’ve definitely found your niche.

2. Android Device / iOS

Not just any device; you need either Android or iOS to start a WhatsApp TV. Preferably, you get a Samsung device or any iOS device, considering the RAM and ROM of the device. The RAM should be at least 3–4 GB, and the internal storage should be reasonable enough.

3. Registered Phone Number

One of the key requirements to create a WhatsApp account is having a registered phone number that you have registered yourself. It’s recommended that you use the number you registered with your details to avoid issues with retrieving a lost SIM when it gets stolen or misplaced.

4. Whatsapp Business Apk

Not just any WhatsApp; it’s recommended you use WhatsApp Business. You can use normal WhatsApp, but the difference is that WhatsApp Business is specially designed for business. It allows you to set up

  • Logo appearance
  • Option to set up your business details
  • Message Statistics
  • Automated greetings and away messages
  • Quick reply templates
  • Group chat with meaningful labels
  • Registration with a landline number
  • Catalogs

While normal WhatsApp doesn’t have these features, Not to worry, WhatsApp Business can be installed alongside the normal WhatsApp application. You can use normal WhatsApp but won’t be able to use some features like auto-reply, catalog, etc. If you decide to use normal WhatsApp, then you can use the Autoresponder Apk to create an auto-reply.

5. Brand Name & Logo

Once a decision has been made on a business niche, you have to think of a brand name that will represent your business. Think of a catchy name that captures the purpose of the WhatsApp TV perfectly. A very memorable name that is not too long and easy to pronounce.

Going professional, it’s recommended that you create a logo for your business to make it stand out and look professional. Humans are visual beings, so more people would respond if your images or videos were as visually appealing as possible. You should also decide which colors you want for your brand identity. The logos should be created with those colors.

In case you don’t have the money or skills it takes to design one, you can open Freelogodesign.org and enter your TV name and the category “MEDIA/EENTERTAINMENT” in the space provided, and it will automatically design the logo for you. After successfully getting your preferred logo, download and save it for use.

6. image & Video Editing Apks

Editing pictures, memes, or creating banners or flyers is a very important thing you need to do to mark up or design your pictures to suit your needs. There are tons of picture editing apps, to mention but a few: Pixellab, PicsArt Pro, Canva, etc.

Creating stunning videos to post online seems to be the best. Always add your trademark (watermark) to each of your contents. Examples of video editing apps on Android and iOS are Inshot Pro, CapCut, LumaFusion (iOS), KineMaster Pro (Android), iMovie (iOS), FilmoraGo (Android), Apple Clips (iOS), and Filmmaker Pro (iOS).

7. Backup (Gmail or iCloud)

WhatsApp TV entails saving lots of contacts on your device. There is a limit to the number of contacts a SIM can store, and storing contacts on a device is not advisable in case you lose the device. The best method to save your contacts for easy recovery is to save and back up your contacts to Gmail (Android) or iCloud (ios).

Gmail: On your Android device, open your phone contact apk. Tap on Settings and change the default saving location to your Gmail account. If you have tons of contacts that were not saved on your Gmail account before, open your Gmail account, go to Manage Account, and backup your contacts.

iCloud: When you set up iCloud for Contacts, your contacts are stored in the cloud instead of locally on your device. Go to Settings > [your name] > iCloud. Turn on Contacts, When you’re asked if you want to merge or cancel, tap merge.

Benefits of Running a WhatsApp TV 

Running a WhatsApp TV has a lot of benefits attached to it, depending on the niche you want to go into. Among the benefits of running a WhatsApp TV are:

Increase in Sales: When you create a WhatsApp TV for the purpose of selling a certain good with your status, as you save more contacts and your views keep increasing, your business will keep growing, which will in turn increase your sales.

Business Marketing/Promoting: When you create a WhatsApp TV just to take ads from business owners and promote their businesses or just to entertain your viewers, people will come to you to advertise their businesses on your status as they keep seeing your growth increase through screenshots of your views posted on your status. The more they advertise on your TV status, the more you earn.

Increase in Contacts: The more you post on your saved contacts or on your WhatsApp status with your business name and number watermark to images or videos, the more contacts you gain. As people keep sharing any content you post that catches their interest, the more interested you get.

How to Create a WhatsApp TV and Monetize It

Yes, you are there. It’s time to create and start running a professional WhatsApp TV. Read the below guide carefully and don’t miss any steps.

Create WhatsApp Account

  • Download WhatsApp Business app, install and open it.
  • You will be prompt to enter your phone number and verify with the OTP sent to the number.
  • After that, Fill out your business name, select a business category, and choose a profile picture.
  • Wait for the setup to complete, then open your WhatsApp business.

After successfully creating a WhatsApp business account, the next thing is to set catalog, auto-reply, create link, etc. for your business under WhatsApp Business Tools.

Explore WhatsApp Business Tools

The WhatsApp Business app has several tools to help you run your business efficiently. To explore these tools, go to your chat screen. Tap More Options on Android or Settings on iPhone. Then, tap Business Tools.

Catalog: This tool helps you showcase and share your products and services within your business account. If you intend to sell goods with your WhatsApp TV, you can use this to display images of your products, name, and even price attached to each product, as well as share the catalog link.

Short link: Make it easy for new customers to connect with you. Share the auto-generated short link with new customers to enable them to message your business. Learn how to use short links on Android or iPhone.

Messaging tools (Auto Reply): Use WhatsApp messaging templates to build key conversations in advance. Create automated greeting messages to engage with new customers. Set away messages to let customers know when you’ll be back, or build quick replies to answer frequently asked questions.

Labels: This helps to sort your conversations with customers into helpful categories, like “new customers” or “pending orders.”

After setting up the necessary things, the next thing is to start promoting your WhatsApp TV to get more contacts to add up and, in turn, get more views.

Promote WhatsApp Tv or Increase WhatsApp Status View

To increase your WhatsApp status views, you need to first create a personalized link with your numbers so as to direct your users on what to do when they click on your link. An example of a personalized link is:

https://api.whatsapp.com/send?phone=2348140106758&text=Hi,%20my%20name%20is%20

This personalized link will ask any user that taps on it to enter their name.

You can enter any text and make sure to put %20 after each word, and you can also edit the number to your liking. If you found it difficult to edit here, you can visit here and create your personalized link. Now, to promote your WhatsApp TV, there are several ways in which you can go about that.

Social media audience: social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter are great tools to increase your status views. Share your TV link in Facebook groups, under the comments of popular Facebook pages, etc.

Promotion: With the right promotion and the right audience, it’s easier to increase your status views. Pay other WhatsApp TV owners so they can advertise your link on their status; in return, you get more contacts, and more contacts lead to more status views!

Contact Gain: This is one of the easiest ways to increase your view. You create a WhatsApp group and share it on your status with a quote telling them, “If you want to get up to 500 views daily, tap the group link to join.” Once they join, you task them to either repost the group link on their status or any of the groups they are in, and they should send you proof. Once they send you proof, you save their contact information.

NOTE: Create a new Gmail account and use it to save those contacts. Once you save it, ensure you save your contact information on thail. Noexportrt the contact to a VCF file and send it to the group, informing them to click on the VCF file and import it to their phone or Gmail account (recommended).

Referral Contest: Hosting a referral contest is another great way to get people busy with your TV link. You give them a task, asking them to share your TV link with their friends and loved ones, and whoever sends you more contacts is declared the winner (of course, a price is involved).

Giveaways: Lots of people love freebies; they do anything just to win things for free. Once in a while, you may do a cash or airtime giveaway on your WhatsApp TV. This attracts more people to view your status.

Posting good content: Post educational and informational content, funny content, etc. will make users glued to view your status.

Monetize WhatsApp Tv Status

You can monetize your WhatsApp TV in various ways, which can increase your daily earnings as you keep getting more views.

Start Placing Advert on your WhatsApp Tv

You can monetize your WhatsApp status by running ads for people and getting paid in return. But this is only possible when you have more status viewers.

As a WhatsApp TV owner, your only chance of making money is when you have lots of status views (people viewing your status). “You can’t have just 35–60 status views and expect anyone to pay you for an advertisement.”

But in cases where you get up to 100–5,000 status views within 24 hours, you can be making at least N20,000 daily from advertisements. You charge your advertiser a certain amount per day and multiply it by the number of days they wish to advertise with you. Giving discounts on your advertising plan draws more advertisers.

Earn commissions through affiliate marketing or referral programs.

You can start referring your viewers to join any paying affiliate or referral program and earn some commission in return. The more you earn and post proof of payment, anyone interested will join and also make money through you. In turn, this will grow your business and bank account as you get more people to refer, and they will also earn through you.

What you need to do is write a short, interesting article about the affiliate or referral program and include your affiliate or referral link. The article should be written in such a way that it promotes the product. So it will trigger your audience to buy the product or register for the referral program to make money. And you’ll be earning commissions for doing SO.

Drawing Traffic to Website

If you have a website that you have monetized with any monetizing sites like AdSense, Monetizemore, etc. When you create an article and post it on your status to help them with one or two things, if the article turns out to be what your viewers are looking for, they are likely to click on the link and head on to your website.

The more they visit your website, the more you make money from it. This, in simple terms, means getting paid for writing. You write and get paid for the idea you have composed in your article, which in turn helps those in need of it.

Start Selling Your Product

If you’ve got a product to sell, this is a great opportunity to make your business sell more effectively. Some of your status viewers will be interested in your product. Your products can include clothes, wigs, Islamic wear, skin care products, etc.

You can even offer a different product at the same time to boost your chances of getting sales. Services like recharge cards and data are always in high demand.

And if you’re not offering these services, you can easily opt into offering recharge and data services to your followers.

Offer Online Classes to Your Whatsapp TV followers

Online classes are also one of the ways to make money with your WhatsApp TV. You can just create premium classes for your viewers who have an interest in what you want to teach.

Depending on what you want to teach, you can always decide how much they’ll pay you.

Conclusion

WhatsApp TV is one of the smartest ways to start making money online. However, you should know that there are certain steps you should take if you want to be successful.

If you’ve been looking for ways to start making money with your smartphone, then this is an opportunity for you. You should know that you have to post regularly so that your audience won’t get bored.
